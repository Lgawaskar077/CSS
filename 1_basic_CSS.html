Story 2: The Clever Crow

One hot summer day, a thirsty crow was flying in search of water. After a long time, it saw a pot with little water at the bottom. The crow tried to drink but could not reach the water. Instead of giving up, it got an idea. The crow picked up small stones and dropped them into the pot one by one. Slowly, the water level rose. Soon, the crow was able to drink the water and flew away happily.

A New Story (About 200 Words)

Aarav lived in a small village, where every evening he sat by the river and dreamed about the future. His father was a fisherman, and his mother earned a living by sewing clothes. Their house was small, but their dreams were big. Aarav loved reading books and believed that words had the power to change the world One year, a severe drought hit the village. The river began to dry up, fish disappeared, and worry spread across the villagers’ faces. Aarav felt helpless at first, but then he decided to act. He read old newspapers and books to learn about water conservation. Soon, he gathered the village children and started cleaning the pond. They dug small pits to collect rainwater and went door to door explaining the importance of saving water.
